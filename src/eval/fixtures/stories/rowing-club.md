## Raw notes
Club was 800 quid in the red when I took over as treasurer in second year. The previous treasurer had miscounted the membership fees and booked the wrong minibus company — we were double-paying for a vehicle we weren't even using. The committee wanted to cancel the summer tour to Cambridge. I didn't want to do that because it's the main event of the year and cancelling it would have hurt recruitment. So I went through every line of the budget, renegotiated with the minibus company (got a 15% reduction by committing to two years), ran a second subs collection for members who hadn't paid, and found a local business to sponsor the blades in exchange for logo placement. Closed the gap in six weeks. Tour went ahead, 24 people attended — biggest in four years.
