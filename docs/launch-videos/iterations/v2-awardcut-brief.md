# V2 "Watch It Count" — Award-Cut Design Brief (Option B)

**Goal:** an award-worthy, viral-feeling 15s Instagram Reel that adopts
Reference A's *motion language* but in Cyclops's *brand* — **near-white paper,
amber→gold accent, liquid-gold transitions**. Free, built in Remotion.

## Creative thesis
The dread of repeating yourself 47 times, collapsed into one act of memory,
resolved by one human click. The hero idea: **the "1" becomes the Cyclops eye** —
the single answer, the single eye, the single click. One conceptual payoff that
earns the logo.

## Visual system (from Ref A, re-skinned)
- **Background:** crisp near-white `#fffefb` (not warm linen). High-key, soft
  consistent drop shadows, objects floating in white space.
- **Accent:** amber `#c05f10` → gold `#f0b35f` gradient at reward/premium beats.
  Tiny ink + gold confetti flecks. Amber is the ONLY hue (brand law).
- **Object vocabulary (iconic, not literal UI):** rounded "Why this firm?" cards,
  a **folder fanning files**, **stacked planes in 3D perspective**, a **3D form
  carousel** orbiting an axis, a **progress bar + counting %**, a **checkmark**,
  a **toggle**, a **pill that morphs into a glowing gradient**, a **cursor**,
  the **eye mark**.

## Motion principles (the bar)
1. **New visual idea ~every 1s** — never hold a beat; ~12 distinct shots.
2. **Real 3D + camera moves** — perspective, `preserve-3d`, orbit/push-in; not flat.
3. **Liquid wave swoosh** transitions (curved leading edge + soft shadow + SFX).
4. **Springy overshoot** on entrances; **motion blur** (`@remotion/motion-blur`
   Trail) on fast elements for gloss.
5. **Reward beats:** counting number → checkmark → confetti; pill → glow morph.
6. **Tactile use:** cursor visibly types/clicks/toggles/scrolls.
7. **Sound-synced:** every hit on the 120 BPM grid (beat = 15 frames @ 30fps).

## Beat-by-beat storyboard (15s / 450f / 120 BPM)
| Time | Frames | Beat | Shot |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–1.0 | 0–30 | Hook | White. Amber card slides in from left, flips to reveal "Why this firm?". Counter label + number start ticking 1,2,3,4 to a counting-voice feel. |
| 1.0–2.5 | 30–75 | The pile | Identical cards rain/stack in 3D perspective (bottom-left→top-right), one per beat; counter races to ~47; camera pushes in; frame fills with dread. |
| 2.5–3.0 | 75–90 | Peak | Counter hits ~47, everything jitters, micro-freeze; flicker "…again?" |
| 3.0–3.3 | 90–100 | **Wave** | Liquid amber→gold wave sweeps R→L (swoosh SFX), wiping the pile. |
| 3.3–5.0 | 100–150 | The answer | One card center; cursor types once; card becomes an amber **memory file**; a **folder fans** the one answer out to many destinations. "Answer once." punches. Chime. |
| 5.0–7.0 | 150–210 | 3D carousel | Forms (Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby) **orbit a central axis**; camera orbits; each front-facing form auto-fills with a gradient bar + field tick. "In your voice." |
| 7.0–8.5 | 210–255 | Reward | Orbit resolves into one **progress bar 0→100 with counting %**, snapping to a big amber **checkmark + confetti**. Dopamine. |
| 8.5–10.0 | 255–300 | Tactile | Fast micro-cuts: a **toggle** flips, a list scrolls, a **pill morphs into a glowing gradient**; pinned counter spins DOWN 47→…→3. |
| 10.0–11.0 | 300–330 | Riser | Convergence + riser; counter crawls 3→2; the one Ashby form rises center, answer readable; cursor flies in on an arc. |
| 11.0–12.0 | 330–360 | **Drop** | On the beat-drop: cursor SLAMS Submit; counter slams to **"1"**; confetti explosion; button → "Submitted ✓"; "The 1 is your click." Biggest hit. |
| 12.0–13.5 | 360–405 | Transform | The **"1" morphs into the Cyclops eye** (numeral → iris). Wave swoosh to dark. |
| 13.5–15.0 | 405–450 | Lockup | Dark ink. Eye blinks open; CYCLOPS wordmark; "Never say anything twice." Final chord. Hold. |
