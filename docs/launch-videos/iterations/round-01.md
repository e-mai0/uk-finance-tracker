# Cyclops Launch Reels — Round 1 Scripts (7 versions)

Format target: Instagram Reels, 9:16 vertical, 12–15 seconds, designed for
sound-on but readable sound-off (big on-screen captions).

Product facts the scripts may use (no inventing):
- Cyclops is the AI "application OS" inside Trackr for UK finance summer internships.
- It remembers everything you tell it once — visible, editable memory ("never say anything twice").
- It writes answers and cover letters in YOUR voice, grounded in your real stories (no AI tells, no "I'm excited to apply").
- One-button apply: fills Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby application forms deterministically in under a second; you always review and click submit.
- Tracks every UK finance summer internship: live ingestion, fresh finds, deadlines, 0–100 fit scores with reasons.
- Overnight prep: researches employers for deadline-near roles while you sleep; morning brief waiting in chat.
- Name device: a cyclops has one eye → "One eye on every internship."

Reference styles:
- REF-A (clean motion-graphics reel): white bg, single accent colour, beat-synced
  cuts every 1s, cursor clicks, progress bar, checkmark + confetti, logo sting. ~30s, no VO.
- REF-B (cinematic launch film): "What if…" VO hook → "Meet X." brand moment →
  device close-ups + real human typing → UI payoff montage → question CTA. ~50s, male VO.

---

## V1 — "2:47 AM" (POV pain → relief, UGC-cinematic, REF-B human shots)
Hook (0–2s): Phone-camera shot, dark room lit by laptop. Caption: **"2:47am. Cover letter number 14."**
VO (tired, real): "Every UK finance internship wants to know why you want it. In 300 words. Forty times."
(2–6s): Rapid cuts: blank Word doc cursor blinking; deadline tabs; energy drink can.
(6–10s): Cut to daylight. Same student, calm, one tap on "Apply with Cyclops". Form fills instantly, field by field. Caption: **"It already knows your stories. It writes like you."**
(10–13s): Student reads, smiles, clicks Submit themselves. Caption: **"You just hit submit."**
End card (13–15s): Cyclops eye-logo blink. **"Cyclops. Never say anything twice."**

## V2 — "Watch It Count" (beat-synced motion graphics, REF-A style)
Hook (0–1s): White screen. Huge mono counter ticking fast: **"Questions you'll answer this cycle: 412"** with a hard beat.
(1–4s): Counter explodes into a wall of identical question cards: "Why this firm?" "Describe a time you led…" — stacking to the beat.
(4–7s): A claret cursor clicks ONE card. Whoosh — all duplicate cards collapse into a single glowing memory file.
(7–10s): Progress bar fills 0→100 in one beat. Checkmark + confetti. Caption: **"Answer once. Cyclops remembers forever."**
(10–13s): Grid of ATS forms (Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby) auto-filling simultaneously, sub-second.
End card (13–15s): Eye logo + **"Cyclops — one eye on every internship."**

## V3 — "Why Goldman?" (repetition gag)
Hook (0–2s): Close-up typing: "Why Goldman Sachs?" Caption: **"Application 1."**
(2–5s): Smash cuts, same shot, progressively more dead-eyed: "Why J.P. Morgan?" "Why Barclays?" "Why Citadel?" Captions: **"Application 9… 17… 31."** Speed ramps up until it's a blur.
(5–8s): Record scratch. Black frame. Caption: **"You've answered this. Thirty times."**
(8–12s): Cyclops chat UI: user types the story ONCE. Then split-screen: three different firm forms each get a *different, tailored* answer in the user's voice.
(12–15s): End card: **"Tell Cyclops once. It tells everyone — in your words."** Eye blink logo.

## V4 — "Meet Cyclops" (cinematic launch film, REF-B faithful)
Hook (0–3s): Dark gradient. VO (calm, cinematic): "What if applying to internships took less time than finding them?"
(3–5s): Eye-logo brand moment. VO: "Meet Cyclops."
(5–8s): Laptop close-up: tracker dashboard, fit scores ticking to 94. VO: "It watches every UK finance internship. Scores how well each one fits you. Explains why."
(8–11s): Phone in hand: morning-brief chat thread. VO: "It researches firms overnight. You wake up to a brief."
(11–13s): One-button apply, form fills, human clicks submit. VO: "One button. Your voice. Your click."
(13–15s): End card. VO: "Cyclops. One eye on every internship."

## V5 — "While You Slept" (FOMO hook)
Hook (0–2s): Phone alarm 7:00am POV. Caption: **"POV: you wake up and the work is already done."**
(2–6s): Phone screen: Cyclops morning brief — "Overnight: 3 new roles match you (94, 91, 88). Citadel deadline in 6 days. I've prepped research on all three."
(6–10s): Quick cuts: fresh-finds rail, fit-score pills, deadline calendar. Caption: **"It hunted while you slept."**
(10–13s): Student at breakfast, one tap, reviews a drafted answer, edits one word, submits. Caption: **"Your coursemates are still making spreadsheets."**
(13–15s): End card: **"Cyclops. Wake up ahead."** Eye blink.

## V6 — "The Wall" (deadline anxiety → radar sweep, motion graphics)
Hook (0–2s): Black screen filling with falling deadline chips — "DUE IN 2 DAYS", "CLOSED", "DUE TOMORROW" — piling up like Tetris with rising noise.
(2–5s): Total silence. One claret eye opens in the dark.
(5–9s): Radar sweep: the chaos snaps into a clean sorted tracker — every UK finance internship, deadline-ordered, fit-scored. Caption: **"Every firm. Every deadline. One eye."**
(9–12s): "Fresh finds" pulse: a boutique fund appears the day it posts. Caption: **"It sees roles the day they open."**
(12–15s): End card: **"Cyclops. Nothing slips past it."**

## V7 — "It Doesn't Sound Like a Robot" (anti-AI-slop angle)
Hook (0–2s): A cover letter being typed: "I am excited to apply…" A red buzzer SLAMS. Caption: **"Recruiters can smell this."**
(2–5s): More AI clichés get buzzed: "…leverage my skills…" BZZT. "…fast-paced environment…" BZZT.
(5–9s): Cyclops memory page scrolls: the user's real stories — rowing captain, hackathon, securities module. Caption: **"It learns YOUR stories. YOUR voice."**
(9–12s): Side-by-side: generic AI paragraph vs. Cyclops draft that reads human. The generic one shreds.
(12–15s): End card: **"Cyclops. AI that sounds like you."** Eye blink.
